# Computational Fluid Dynamics & OpenFOAM

We leverage the power of **OpenFOAM** for high-performance computing in fluid dynamics and multiphase systems.

### Specialized Solvers
Most of our CFD development is hosted on our **[MultiForm GitHub](https://github.com/MultiForm-UoN)**. Key solvers include:
*   **Geo-Energy:** Fractured reservoir simulations and CCS modeling.
*   **Poro-mechanics:** Coupled fluid-solid interactions in biological and geological media.
