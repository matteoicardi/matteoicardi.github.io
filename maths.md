# Mathematical Foundations

My work is rooted in rigorous mathematical analysis applied to physical systems. 

### Key Areas
*   **Partial Differential Equations (PDEs):** Analysis of transport equations in heterogeneous media.
*   **Upscaling & Homogenization:** Techniques for deriving macroscopic descriptions from microscopic physics.
*   **Stochastic Numerics:** Development of efficient algorithms for uncertainty quantification.
