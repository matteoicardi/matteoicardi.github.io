# Industrial Applications

Our mathematical research is motivated by and applied to real-world industrial challenges. By combining multiscale modeling with CFD and uncertainty quantification, we provide robust solutions across several sectors.

### Key Industrial Application Areas

- **Energy & Subsurface Exploration:** Modeling fractured reservoirs, Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS), and unconventional risks.
- **Manufacturing & Materials:** Poro-mechanics in advanced composite structures and fusion energy components.
- **Biomedical & Healthcare:** Brain tissue deformation modeling for neurosurgery and thermal ablation for tumor treatment.
- **Food Processing:** Solid-fluid coupled simulations for freezing and thermal processing (e.g., with Mondelez, Cranswick).
- **Chemical Engineering:** Population balance modeling for crystallization, combustion, and multiphase flows.
- **Environment:** Long-term mechanical evolution and ecohydrological modeling of peatlands (MPeat).
- **Battery Technology:** Multiscale degradation and performance modeling for next-generation electrification.
