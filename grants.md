# Research Funding & Projects

Our work is supported by national and international funding bodies, balancing fundamental research with industrial application.

### Major Projects & Funding
- **EU Horizon 2020 (SECURe):** WP Lead on developing data assimilation for fractured reservoirs in carbon capture (CCS).
- **Royal Academy of Engineering & Global Initiatives:** PI on Industrial CCS in Colombia, nanofluid transport, and asphaltene dynamics in subsurface production.
- **UKRI/EPSRC:** Industrial CASE studentship with TOTAL for nanosensing in geological applications.
- **N2N (€546,000):** Co-I. Focused on poro-mechanics in advanced lightweight composite structures.
- **SilentProp:** Co-I. Model reduction for noise generation in electric aircraft.
