# Research Funding & Projects

Our work is supported by national and international funding bodies, balancing fundamental research with industrial application.

---

### Key Projects & Funding

- **2023–2025: MATHPEAT Research Network**  
  **Funder:** Isaac Newton Institute, EPSRC.  
  **Summary:** Mathematical modelling, numerical analysis, continuum mechanics and uncertainty quantification for soft porous materials, focused on peatlands.  
  **Partners:** Network with 50+ participants globally.

- **2022–2024: GHG-Intensive-Industry Medium-Scale Capture and Utilization Solutions Assessment in Colombia**  
  **Funder:** Royal Academy of Engineering.  
  **Summary:** Assessment of CCS potential in the Colombian industrial sector.

- **2020–2023: SilentProp: Assessing Noise Generation in Aircraft with Distributed Electric Propulsion**  
  **Funder:** EU H2020 (Clean Sky 2).  
  **Summary:** Supervision of PhD student on model reduction for CFD.  
  **Partners:** Univ. of Nottingham, Mathelis, Univ. of Bristol, Mejzlik Propellers, KU Leuven.

- **2019: Dimension Reduction for Solid-State Diffusion in Batteries**  
  **Funder:** London Mathematical Society.  
  **Summary:** Undergraduate Summer Research Internship.

- **2019–2021: Asphaltene Dynamics at the Pore-Scale and the Impact on Oil Production at the Field-Scale**  
  **Funder:** Royal Academy of Engineering.  
  **Summary:** Developing fluid dynamics and macroscale transport models for highly viscous organic compounds in porous media, including aggregation and clogging effects.  
  **Partners:** Universidad Nacional de Colombia, Ecopetrol, Equion.

- **2018–2022: Communication through Porous and Heterogeneous Media**  
  **Funder:** TOTAL (+ contribution from EPSRC).  
  **Summary:** Development of fluid-mechanics-based transfer function approaches for nanosensing and molecular communication in geological applications.

- **2018–2022: N2N: European Industrial Doctorate for Advanced, Lightweight and Silent, Multifunctional Composite Structures**  
  **Funder:** EU H2020-MSCA-ITN.  
  **Summary:** Poro-mechanics modelling and simulation of composite material in the acoustic regime.  
  **Partners:** Univ. of Nottingham (UK), Mathelis (FR).

- **2018–2021: SECURe: Subsurface Evaluation of Carbon Capture and Storage and Unconventional Risk**  
  **Funder:** EU H2020-LCE.  
  **Summary:** Efficient multi-continuum modelling and data assimilation approaches for flow, transport and geomechanics in fractured and heterogeneous reservoirs.  
  **Partners:** Over 10 partners including SINTEF (Norway), INIG (Poland), British Geological Survey (UK).

- **2018–2020: Development of a Robust Modelling and Up-Scaling Technique of Nanofluids Transport and Retention for Subsurface Engineering Applications**  
  **Funder:** Royal Academy of Engineering.  
  **Summary:** Pore-scale simulation and upscaling for nano-particle transport and deposition in porous media.  
  **Partners:** Universidad Nacional de Colombia, Ecopetrol.

- **2018–2020: Data Assimilation and Uncertainty Quantification Strategies for Anomalous Transport in Subsurface Flows**  
  **Funder:** Royal Society (International Exchanges).  
  **Summary:** Development of robust model and calibration approaches beyond the advection-dispersion equation.  
  **Partners:** Politecnico di Milano.
