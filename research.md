### Core Research Pillars
1.  **Multiscale Modeling & Coarse-Graining:** Deriving macroscopic equations from microscopic physics, including Basset-type fractional transport models.
2.  **Porous Media & Subsurface Dynamics:** Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS), fractured reservoirs, and contaminant hydrology.
3.  **Uncertainty Quantification (UQ):** Deploying Multilevel Monte Carlo (MLMC) for robust predictions in the presence of sparse data.
4.  **Scientific Machine Learning (SciML):** Integrating physics into neural networks (PINNs) for inverse transport and modeling problems.
